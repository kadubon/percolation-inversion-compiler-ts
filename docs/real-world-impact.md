# Real-World Impact

PIC-TS represents real-world impact as finite Typed Reality Compilation traces
and certificate checks. It does not perform uncontrolled physical actuation,
background crawling, model self-modification, or model-weight updates.

Use operation profiles to choose what is allowed:

- development: dry-run only;
- research: simulation or shadow only;
- controlled: fixture or local test provider evidence;
- production: operator-approved provider dispatch after CCR-style preflight;
- adversarial: unknown budgets and missing certificates block.

Observation evidence remains evidence, not physical outcome proof. Unknown
budgets become residuals rather than zero.
